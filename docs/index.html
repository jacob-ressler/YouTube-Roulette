WHAT IS THIS?

This is YouTube Roulette (or at least that's what I'm calling it). With just a YouTube channel URL, you are able to view an endless stream of random 1-minute snippets from random videos belonging to that channel. Find a snippet you like? Pause the timer and enjoy as much of the video as you want! Find a snippet you want nothing to do with? Go ahead and skip it with the handy little SKIP button!
